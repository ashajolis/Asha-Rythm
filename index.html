The Three Promises You Make to Yourself
PROMISE 01
I will not plan my life from a place of guilt.
No more planning because you feel behind. No more overnight overhauls because one day went wrong. You plan from abundance — from what you have, what's working, and what you want more of.
PROMISE 02
I will use my apps as tools, not as masters.
Roots, Luminary, UGC HQ, Cycle Guide — they serve you. You do not serve them. If a day goes differently than planned, you close the apps and live the day. You update them tomorrow.
PROMISE 03
I will let my cycle lead, not override me.
Some weeks you will film everything and pitch everyone. Some weeks you will do sofa school and batch cook stew. Both are correct. Both are the life.

Flexibility by Design

Your Day Types

Not every day is the same. You have five named day types. Each morning you choose the one that fits your energy, your cycle, and what life needs today.

How to choose your day typeOpen Roots at 5am during your Wizard Hour. Look at your cycle phase. Feel your body. Pick the day type that matches — not the one you think you should have.
🔥
Full Day
All blocks · All output · Follicular or Ovulatory phase
▶
When to use it
Follicular (Days 5–11) or Ovulatory (Days 12–16) — high energy, sharp brain, magnetic presence
When you wake feeling strong, motivated, and clear
What changes
All blocks run at full intensity — Wizard Hour has a real deliverable
2pm block: batch film 3–4 clips back to back, then edit, then send pitches
☀️
Normal Day
Standard rhythm · Most common day type
▶
When to use it
When nothing special is happening — a regular Monday, a regular Wednesday
What it looks like
Wizard Hour: journaling + one content task
2pm: open UGC HQ, do the next task on your pipeline — film, edit, OR pitch
🌤️
Light Day
Core only · Low energy · Late luteal or any hard day
▶
When to use it
Late luteal (Days 22–25) — PMS, fatigue, low mood
Any day you wake up and your body says no
What it looks like
Wizard Hour: just journaling — no content deliverables
2pm: admin only — check pipeline, respond to messages, no filming
🛋️
Cozy Day
Menstrual phase · Body rest · School from the sofa
▶
When to use it
Menstrual phase — Days 1–4 especially Days 1–2 (heaviest)
What it looks like
Wizard Hour: skip or stay in bed — tea, warmth, gentle music
Rest hour: 2 hours minimum. Heating pad. Iron-rich snack.
2pm: UGC HQ closed. No filming. No pitching.
🌳
Out Day
Adventures · Library · Park · The world is the classroom
▶
When to use it
Planned outings — library Wednesday, park day, market trips, errands
What it looks like
Wizard Hour: happens as normal — the outing is later, not an excuse to skip
2pm: UGC HQ stays closed — film life content naturally with your phone
The Shape of Your Week

Weekly Rhythm

Each day has a personality. When you know Monday is a deep-work day and Wednesday is adventure day, you stop fighting the week and start riding it.

Sunday Evening — The Week's Foundation (20 min)Open Cycle Guide → check your phase for the week. Open UGC HQ → see what's due. Open Luminary → prep school days. Open Roots → set the week's 3 priorities. That's it. Close everything. The week is set.

Check cycle phase entering the week. UGC HQ: what's filming or due this week? Luminary: is school set? Roots: batch cook if follicular. Set 3 weekly priorities. Evening is family time. No work after 7pm Sunday.

The Foundation Everything Rests On

My Rules

These are not suggestions. These are the agreements you have made with yourself. Come back to this page when you feel scattered.

"Consistency is a quiet, daily act. No one sees it happening. Everyone sees the result."
RULE 01
The Wizard Hour is sacred. 5am happens even on hard days.
Even 20 minutes. Even just tea and a journal. The act of showing up for yourself before anyone else does is the most powerful thing you can do. It builds the identity of a woman who keeps her word to herself.
RULE 02
One app at a time. Never two.
When Luminary is open, UGC HQ is closed. When UGC HQ is open, Luminary is closed. When you're with your girls, everything is closed. App-switching is the root of feeling overwhelmed and scattered.
RULE 03
UGC HQ is a 2pm business. It does not exist before or after.
No checking brand emails at 9am. No scrolling your gig tracker at 9pm. Your business runs in one intentional block per day.
RULE 04
Sunday planning replaces all mid-week anxiety.
20 minutes on Sunday — cycle check, UGC pipeline, school prep, Roots priorities. When Sunday is done, the week is decided. You don't re-decide it on Monday morning.
RULE 05
Your body is always right. Your cycle is always correct.
Adjust the plan to match the body. Never adjust the body to match the plan.
RULE 06
The girls come before content. Always.
Your girls will remember your presence, not your productivity. The content will always be there tomorrow. This moment with them will not.
RULE 07
A small done day is better than a perfect planned day.
You do not need a perfect day. You need a real day. Done always beats perfect.
RULE 08
Rest is not earned. Rest is required.
You do not earn your rest hour by being productive enough. Rest is what makes everything else possible.
RULE 09
You are allowed to enjoy your life.
Cook the meal you actually want to eat. Watch the show. Take the walk. Living fully is not a distraction from your goals — it is the source of everything you create.
RULE 10
You have already built more than most people will. Now live inside it.
Roots Flow. Luminary Homeschool. AshaJolies UGC HQ. Your Cycle Life Guide. You built extraordinary infrastructure for your life. The work now is to trust what you built and live fully inside it.

When You Feel Lost

Some days the whole system feels like too much. Everything is behind. Nothing is working. This page is for those days.

"I am not behind. I am not failing. I am a woman in the middle of building something real — and that is always messy in the middle."
Read this out loud right now
The 5-Step Reset — Do These in Order
1️⃣
Put the phone down. Walk away for 10 minutes.Overwhelm lives in the apps and the screen. Make tea. Step outside. Breathe. Come back calmer.
2️⃣
Open your Cycle Guide ONLY. Check what day you're on.That number explains 80% of how you're feeling. In late luteal? This is biological, not failure.
3️⃣
Ask: What is the ONE thing that must happen today?Not the list. Not everything. One thing. Write it down. Just that one thing gets done today.
4️⃣
Cancel, reduce, or postpone everything non-essential.If you're in late luteal or menstrual: drop 60% of the plan. Minimum viable everything.
5️⃣
Reset on Sunday. Not today.Do not try to reorganise your whole life mid-week. Survive today simply. The week can hold a few imperfect days.
"Everything does not have to be perfect for life to be beautiful. Most of the best moments happen inside the imperfect ordinary days."
